Hands-on Workshop

Cyber Security Club, ADIT hosted a One Day Hands-On Workshop on Cybersecurity on 8th April 2023 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM with insights on various topic of the Cybersecurity Domain. 59 students actively participated in the workshop. The guest speaker for the workshop was Dr. Ravi Sheth. The workshop was organized under the guidance of Dr. Krunal Patel & Dr. Narendrasinh Chauhan. The workshop started by felicitating our guest Dr. Ravi Sheth. A very energetic and motivating speech was delivered by Dr. Narendrasinh Chauhan. Dr. Ravi Sheth started the workshop by giving a brief introduction of himself. Installation of Kali Linux was done by the participants as the workshop was based on it.

Basic knowledge of Kali commands was given by our speaker. Live demo & practicals were performed by the participants. Lunch break of 12:00 PM to 1:00 PM was given to the participants. After lunch break, Implementation of Kali in real life was explained by our speaker.
